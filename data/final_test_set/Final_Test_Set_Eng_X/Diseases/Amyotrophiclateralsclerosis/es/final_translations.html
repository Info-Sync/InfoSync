Specialty
  • Neurology
Symptoms
  • Temporary: muscle stiffness, involuntary muscle contractions, gradually increasing weakness | Late: speech difficulty, swallowing and breathing problems; respiratory failure
Common Home
  • Between 50 and 70 years old
Causes
  • Unknown (most), Genetics
Diagnosis
  • Based on symptoms and a brain magnetic resonance
Treatment
  • Non-invasive ventilation, physiotherapy, speech therapy
Medication
  • Riluzol, edaravone
Forecast
  • Life expectancy from 2 to 4 years
Frequency
  • 2.6 cases per 100 000 (in Europe)
Synonyms
  • Lou Gehrig disease | Charcot disease | motor neuron disease
